Why We Exist

Disrupting the Traditional Agriculture Supply Chain

Traditional agricultural marketing in India relies on long chains of intermediaries, leading to massive inefficiencies, poor margins for farmers, and lack of transparency for buyers.

The Traditional Mandi System

  • Multiple Intermediaries: Up to 5-7 brokers between farm-gate and kitchen sink.
  • Heavy Margin Erosion: Farmers receive only 20-30% of what the end buyer pays.
  • No Price Transparency: Opaque pricing driven by local cartel traders.
  • No Traceability: Buyers have no idea which farm or village grew their food.

The AgriLink Model

  • Direct Marketplace: Discovery portal for direct farm-to-business contact.
  • Zero Middleman Fees: 100% of the negotiated price goes straight to the farmer.
  • Verified Profiles: Direct ratings, verified farm locations, and photos.
  • High Traceability: Build long-term repeat relationships with actual growers.

How the Platform Functions

Understanding the direct discovery model, credentials, safety checks, and negotiation rules.

For Farmers & FPOs

1. SMS OTP Authentication

Farmers register using a mobile phone number. A secure OTP is sent via SMS, instantly verifying their active communication line.

2. ID & Farm Verification

Our field agents verify the farmer's credentials by matching their profile with land holdings or FPO member records. Verified farms get a green check badge.

3. List Available Harvest

Enter crop variety, harvest dates, quantities ready or upcoming, price range, and upload geotagged photos showing quality.

4. Handle Inquiries Directly

Interested bulk purchasers will call or WhatsApp you. There is no platform intermediary; you negotiate delivery and price directly.

5. Secure Dispatch

Pack the produce, load logistics (usually organized by the buyer), receive payment direct to bank account or cash, and dispatch.

For Bulk Buyers

1. Create Profile

Register as a commercial entity (Hotel, Restaurant, Retailer, Caterer) with OTP verification and basic business credentials.

2. Filter Directory Search

Filter by crop type, geographic distance, and volume requirements. Review geotagged farm photos and historical seller ratings.

3. Direct Inquiry

Open the farmer profile and click to request contact details. Tap to dial or send a structured WhatsApp message describing your requirements.

4. Close Negotiations

Agree on price per kg, loading logistics, packing standards, and payment terms directly with the farmer or FPO leader.

5. Receive and Rate

Receive shipment at your loading deck, inspect quality, finalize direct payment, and log back onto the platform to rate the grower's service.

Project Mission & Roadmap

Cutting out intermediaries, empowering agricultural entrepreneurs, and creating transparency.

Executive Summary

In the traditional Indian agri-supply chain, farmers suffer from severe margin erosion due to an over-reliance on local traders and commission agents (brokers). Simultaneously, institutional buyers (caterers, retail sellers, hotels) face highly fluctuating prices, lack of quality consistency, and zero trace history of the food they buy.

AgriLink Marketplace solves this problem by utilizing a digital directory infrastructure. We provide verified discovery without taking transactions online. This allows participants to build local direct business loops with no transactional platform fees.

Why Now?

While agri-commerce platform models exist, most inject themselves directly into transaction processing, payments, and complex shipping setups. This adds platform fee percentages, limits local transport flexibility, and isolates smallholders who struggle with digital escrow systems.

Mobile networks, combined with high WhatsApp and digital payment (UPI) penetration in rural regions, make direct, offline-settled deals highly efficient. The market is ready for a pure discovery infrastructure focused on trust, ID validation, and location data.
